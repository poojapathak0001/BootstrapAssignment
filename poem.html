Evil

I hate this place,
And yet I’m here:
Just to see your face.
You were laughing just now with your new love interest,
And now it have already taken pace.

Just to see you suffer,
As much as you made me do,
I’ll stick around in your downfall.
Pretend,
as if I’ll catch you,
And right before the evil can touch my arms,
I’ll let the concrete hit you.

It’s not karma,
It’s just me: the masochist that you made me be.
I was right, you were a sakura,
A bloodflower,
Only when I touched, the venom spread,
I saw the demon soul mock my chained heart.
